About Us

AU English & IT Academy provides transformative training for learners aiming to work or study in international tech environments. Our courses blend technical skills with English fluency to help you unlock global opportunities.

  • 💡 Expert tutors in both English and IT fields

Our mission is to empower students with the skills, confidence, and global mindset to thrive in the tech world.
